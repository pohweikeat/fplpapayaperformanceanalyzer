The Only Triple Gameweek Ever
2020/21 GW35 — Manchester United played three times in a single gameweek. 8 other teams doubled. 15 total fixtures. A freak of the COVID-disrupted season that has never been repeated. Lau Guan Zhen topped the league with 134 pts that week.

2022/23 GW7 · Not a real record
The Queen's Gameweek
Queen Elizabeth II passed away on 8 September 2022. All 10 Premier League fixtures that weekend were postponed. Every manager in every FPL league scored 0 points. This GW is excluded from all individual records on this site.
